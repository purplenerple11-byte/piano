How this works

Each phrase below is a short line. Press Start and play it as the cursor reaches each note — right notes turn green, wrong ones red, and if a position won't stick, turn on 💡 Hints and every key you play shows up on the staff with its letter. Get a phrase comfortable, then move to the next.

When the phrases are all landing, drop to The whole thing at the bottom and play them back to back. Keep feedback on while you're still learning it; when you want a real run, hit 🙈 Feedback off — it stops marking you as you go and just shows your score at the end, the way a performance actually feels.
